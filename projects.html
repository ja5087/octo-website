BerkeleyTime

BerkeleyTime is a course discovery and planning tool which combines all of Berkeley's academic information in one place. Tens of thousands of Berkeley students use BerkeleyTime every semester to plan their class schedule, filter by breadth and college requirements, look up historical enrollment trends, and view previous grade distributions.

Beehive

Beehive connects students to research positions year-round. It matches aspiring engineers with hands-on investigation and invention in a range of disciplines. Faculty and graduate students can post openings, while students can browse the listings to find positions and projects that match their interests and skills.

API Project

The API Project leads OCTO's initiatives in creating open APIs and curating data for student developers. Currently it is working on aggregating data related to degree planning and answering questions like "Given that I have taken x classes, which majors do I qualify for?"
